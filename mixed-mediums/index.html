Mixed Mediums

Kinetic Motor Movement

During a kinetic sculpture course at Illinois State, I created a vehicular type structure that moved based on a vibration propulsor. As the motor moves, a weighted piece of pewter causes an axis to swing up and down. The force created causes the mechanical structure to shift back and forth and even propel itself forward.

LED Graduation Cap

To celebrate college graduation, I decided to have a little fun with my graduation cap. I decided on coding LED lights with an Arduino UNO to put on a black cloth that I would then sew onto my graduation cap. Personally, I'm a huge fan of Harry Potter and I found that magic was the perfect catalyst to cause sparks of light on my hat. Before adding the LEDs however, I sewed a wand to the black cloth and then spray painted "Mischief Managed" to complete the Potter-esque cap.

Intaglio

Intaglio is an Italian form of printmaking and is known as the opposite of relief printing. Rather than the raised surfaces holding ink, it is the sunken areas that hold ink. While studying intaglio at ISU, I created a series of tryptich self-portraits focusing on my celtic ancestry but my favorite piece was a still life I made of assorted fruits and veggies.
