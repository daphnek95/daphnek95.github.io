I have always had a love for enamel pins and designing them. When I was younger I saw a Disney cast member hard at work in one of the shops, sketching out a new pin design. I loved seeing them bring Snow White to life on the paper. In college, I finally decided to tackle my love for pins by sketching my own designs. I eventually saved up enough money and produced my first design.

For pins, I start out with sketches and then drop them over into Illustrator. I would love to produce more but until my wallet can handle that, I'm content with drawing different designs.
