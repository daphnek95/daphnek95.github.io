Leap Motion Projection on Water

For a final project in a processing course at Illinois State, I designed a galaxy themed game that switched between two different states. To play both games, the program would track your index finger while the game was being projected on top of water.

Cuddlepants - Wearable Technology

This was one of my first coding projects/interactive installations at Illinois State University. For class, the goal was to work in pairs of two to create a piece of wearable technology. At the time, my partner Cassidy and I learned about professional cuddlers in Los Angeles, California. We were so amused with the idea that we decided to create wearable technology that took the intimacy and personal touch out of the cuddling process. When someone rests their head on the pant's pillow, a mechanical arm swings over and strokes the user's head.
