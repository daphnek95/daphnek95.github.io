Graduation Cap

For me I saw my college graduation cap as the last final project for my Arts Technology degree. I decided to code LEDs into my cap and it evolved into a Harry Potter themed piece. The LEDs were used to create the magic being produced from a wand.

I started off with coding the LEDs using Arduino. Then I moved on to cutting out a piece of fabric the shape and size of my graduation cap, sketched and stitched a wand onto the fabric, spray painted the words "Mischief Managed" and then glued LEDs into the fabric. After the LEDs were secured I soldered them to wires. Then the tricky part came, I had to sew the fabric onto my graduation cap, moving the wires so they would be hiddn towards the back of my cap. I created a hidden pouch underneath my graduation cap that stored my Arduino which acted as the power source for the LEDs.
