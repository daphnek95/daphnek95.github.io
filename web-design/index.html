Discovering code has been one of the greatest things to happen to me as an artist. I fell in love with web design and the whole web development process. My degree as an Arts Technology major as well as my time as a Front-End Web Designer for Illinois State University has helped develop and grow my passion and strengths as a web designer. I am versed in HTML, CSS, SASS, and JQuery.

Below are websites I have designed along with wireframes and mockups.
