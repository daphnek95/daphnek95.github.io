During college, I was a part of the University Program Board (UPB). The particular committee I was involved in was the graphics committee. The team was in charge of designing posters and advertisements for university held events.

Usually committee members were able to choose which events they wanted to design graphics for and other times they were assigned. Each event was different, so it was a great opportunity to adapt to the needs of the event and create a piece that fits it well. The graphics were all made using Illustrator.
