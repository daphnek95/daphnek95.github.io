University Program Board -- Graphic Design Committee

The UPB (University Program Board) at Illinois State University is in charge of creating various events around campus to unite students together and have some fun. It was my responsibility as a graphics committee member to design various advertisements for events. These would range from posters and handbills, to button pins and signs displayed around campus television screens. When I'm not assigned an event, I'm there working with my team, critiquing and guiding others in their designs.
