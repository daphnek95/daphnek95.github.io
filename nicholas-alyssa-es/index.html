Nicholas and Alyssa are such a fun and quirky couple, so it's only fitting that their engagement session was fun and a little different. We waited for fresh snow to fall before sneaking in a quick session. The couple was freezing but we managed to snag some beautiful shots!

It was pretty clear from the session just how much these two love each other. The way they were in sync and made each other laugh was incredible to witness. I wish the two of them the best of luck for their wedding; I love them dearly and know their marriage will be an adventure!
