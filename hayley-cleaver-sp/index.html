Hayley Cleaver is a sweet senior at Pekin Community High School. She was such a pleasure to take pictures of and together we made some pretty adorable photos!

She was a real trooper, following me to three different locations. First we showed off her school pride at an old abandoned orphanage. We did a quick outfit change to do some shots with some more casual clothes. Then we journeyed downtown for a more cozy feel. After that we changed to a dressier outfit and took her to a local horticulture center just in time to make Golden Hour!
