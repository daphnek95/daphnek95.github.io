Hello!

My name is Daphne Kraft and I am an Arts Technology major at Illinois State University. While I have dipped my toes in many different digital mediums, my passions lie within photography and sound design.

Photography

Wielding a Nikon D80, I'm always on a mission to capture the world around me. Types of photography I have dabbled with: photography, food, portraits, and animal.

Graphic Design

Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator are my power tools. I like to create posters, tee shirt designs, logos, and business cards.

Web Design

With code always changing, I like the learning curve and the education that comes with designing a website for a company or persons specific needs. It all starts with a sketch before becoming a successful webpage.

Sound Design

A fast and growing love of mine. I enjoy editing music just as much as I enjoy creating stories through the use of sound effects.

Web Video

With only the video capabilites of my iPhone and Adobe Premiere Pro, I love capturing and creating stories for others to enjoy. Especially creepy tales.
