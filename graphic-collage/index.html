Graphic collages are currently my favorite form of design work. Weaving different pictures and graphic elements together to form one cohesive and meaningful piece is a rewarding challenge.

For these pieces I search through public domain images to find ones that stand out or inspire me. As I shuffle through my collection of images, an idea builds and I take the work over to Photoshop. It takes a lot of layers and masks to accomplish a piece to create one cohesive piece.
