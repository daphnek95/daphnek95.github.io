JUNE 16, 1995

On this day, the number one song on the US music charts was none other than Have You Really Loved A Woman by Bryan Adams.

Bryan Adams

This particular song was rated number one in the United States for five weeks. Bryan Adams' song was featured in the movie Don Juan DeMarco which stared Johnny Depp.
