Paper Planes is a pop rock band that started out at Illinois State University.

When Paper Planes decided to enter a Battle of the Bands concert at Illinois State they reached out and hired me as their photographer for the event. Before the concert we took some group shots. Then when the concert started, I bounced around the stage and through the crowd to get some great shots of the band. It was a fun night and a great experience!
